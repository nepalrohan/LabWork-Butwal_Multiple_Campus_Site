About Us

Butwal Multiple Campus is one of the leading educational institutions located in Butwal, Nepal. It offers a wide range of programs and opportunities for students to excel in their academic and professional careers. In addition to its academic excellence, the campus is committed to the holistic development of students, encouraging them to engage in a variety of extracurricular activities. These activities range from sports and arts to leadership programs and community service projects. The aim is to create well-rounded individuals who are not only academically proficient but also socially responsible and active contributors to society. The campus also maintains strong partnerships with national and international organizations, providing students with ample opportunities for internships, research projects, and employment after graduation.
